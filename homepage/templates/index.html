    How does Candor work?

    When you get stuck in an apartment contract Candor is a tool that finds a tenant to take it and sells it to them.

    The best part is you only pay Candor if we sell it for you!

    Sell your contract now

    Sell your contract in three easy steps:

    Post your contract
    To post your contract all you have to do is answer 7 basic questions and give us a bit of info so we can keep in touch.
    We sell it for you
    Kick back and relax and let candor do the work matching you to a buyer, we'll shoot you an email when there is a match.
    Pay $20 when it sells
    The best part is you only pay when it is sold, and you can cancel at any time.
